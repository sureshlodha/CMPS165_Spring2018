UK Immigration aims to look at the influx of people coming into the United Kingdom and see who is coming into the United Kingdom and why. The visualizations display long term and short term movement through the United Kingdom through visa information as well as general population data. If we continued the project we would have liked to relate the data more to Brexit, when the United Kingdom left the European Union, and other topical events that may have influenced movement in the region.

The regions displayed here are generally limited to South Asia, East Asia, Europe (non-EU), Sub-Saharan Africa, Middle East, and South East Asia. All other regions are colored as Other. The regions which are included therein are: Oceania, North America, North Africa, The EU, Central Asia, Central and South America.
